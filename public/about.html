About

Forward Motion Software is a group founded by Hassan Anees, Jonas Bakelaar, Ian Bennett, Anthony Candelino, Gio Diamante, and Cameron Dixon. We believe our Salary Analyzer provides users with a simple, intuitive interface that allows them to access the data they need. Our software allows users to view salary data from different sources around Canada, download a CSV of the data, view statistical analyses of the data, and view graphs of the data. We retrieve our data from official government sources from British Columbia, Ontario, and Alberta.
